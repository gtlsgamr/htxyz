Of cloudy skies and petrichor,      
Thirty days an eternity.      
'twas the best month of them all.      
Of cloudy skies and petrichor,      
and bruised ribs and broken bones      
The sun was all that we could see.      
Of cloudy skies and petrichor,      
Thirty days an eternity.
