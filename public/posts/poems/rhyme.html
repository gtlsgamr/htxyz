rhyme

2021-12-08

Two quick beats,
miss a step
then repeat.
My heart does that
when your touch
it needs.
I cry and I
Plead, and I
Beg on my knees,
But all you do
is laugh at me and lie
and deceive

I hope that you talk
and I could tell you
What I need

But jokes
and insults
are all I recieve.
Two quick beats
miss a step
Then repeat
I wish I could
Stop acting
Like a little kid.
