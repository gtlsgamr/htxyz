heaven

2021-12-08

I miss being a kid
my heart under a lid, covered.
no ache, and no lovers,
no secrets to uncover. I missed
being naive at least.
between shadows and mist, I played
not a moment in dismay.
and then my head I laid, in heaven
my mother’s lap.
