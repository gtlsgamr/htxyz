kanye

2021-12-08

I can’t help but hear
The soundtrack to my self destruction
pulling me in with tremendous suction.
I can’t help but hear
The very voice of my deepest fears
Rapping over some dope production.
I can’t help but hear
The soundtrack to my self destruction.
